Research Interests

My research interests focus on identifying and addressing security threats and privacy leakage in networked and distributed computing systems, specifically in IoT security, machine learning security & privacy. I am also interested in mobile/wearable sensing for applications like drone sensing and networking, mobile interaction.

I am actively looking for self-motivated and talented students/research associates to join my research group. You could choose the below research topics (but not limited to)

  • Security and Privacy for IoT Systems
  • Machine Learning Security & Privacy
  • Mobile/Wearable Sensing
